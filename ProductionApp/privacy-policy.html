Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This app processes all audio and text data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Audio Data

Whisper Control Mobile processes audio data that you:

  • Record directly through the app's microphone
  • Import from your device's file system
  • Import from Photos or other apps

Important: All audio processing occurs locally on your device using CoreML and OpenAI's Whisper models. No audio data is transmitted to external servers.

1.2 Transcribed Text

The app generates text transcriptions from your audio files. These transcriptions are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Processed locally for RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) features
  • Never transmitted to external services

1.3 Document Data

When you import documents (PDFs, text files) for RAG processing:

  • Documents are processed locally on your device
  • Text extraction happens on-device
  • No document content is sent to external servers

1.4 Device Information

The app may collect minimal device information necessary for functionality:

  • Device model and iOS version (for compatibility)
  • App usage statistics (processed locally)
  • File system access permissions (as granted by you)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Processing Only

All data processing occurs on your device:

  • Audio Transcription: Using on-device Whisper models via CoreML
  • RAG Processing: Local vector embeddings and search
  • File Management: Local storage and organization
  • Export Features: Local file generation and sharing

2.2 No External Data Transmission

We do not:

  • Send your audio files to external servers
  • Transmit transcribed text to third parties
  • Upload your documents to cloud services
  • Share your data with advertisers or analytics companies

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device:

  • Audio files are stored in the app's local directory
  • Transcriptions are saved in local SQLite databases
  • Document embeddings are stored locally for RAG functionality
  • All data is protected by iOS sandboxing and encryption

3.2 Data Encryption

Your data benefits from:

  • iOS device encryption at rest
  • App sandboxing for additional security
  • Secure file system permissions

4. Permissions and Access

4.1 Required Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone Access: Required for audio recording and live transcription
  • Speech Recognition: Used for Apple's live transcription features
  • File System Access: For importing and exporting audio files
  • Photos Library Access: To import audio/video files from Photos

4.2 Permission Control

You can revoke these permissions at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security. Note that revoking permissions may limit app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OpenAI Whisper Models

The app uses OpenAI's Whisper models for transcription, but these models are:

  • Downloaded and stored locally on your device
  • Processed entirely on-device via CoreML
  • Not connected to OpenAI's servers during use

5.2 Apple Services

The app may use Apple's built-in services:

  • Speech Recognition (for live transcription)
  • File System APIs (for document import/export)
  • CoreML (for on-device AI processing)

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to:

  • Export transcriptions via AirDrop, Messages, or Mail
  • Share files through iOS sharing mechanisms
  • Back up your device to iCloud (subject to your iCloud settings)

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained:

  • Locally on your device until you delete it
  • According to your device's storage management
  • Subject to your iCloud backup settings (if enabled)

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

This privacy policy applies to users worldwide. Since all data processing occurs locally on your device, your data does not cross international borders unless you choose to share it through iOS sharing mechanisms.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • iOS Privacy Requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) principles
